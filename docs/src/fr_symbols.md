# F/R Symbols

The F/R layer builds equation systems for multiplicity-free fusion rules and
stores exact F/R data.

Representative functions:

- `fr_equation_system`
- `pentagon_equations`, `hexagon_equations`
- `validate_fr_system`
- `FRData`, `F_symbol`, `R_symbol`
- `semion_fr_data`, `fibonacci_fr_data`, `ising_fr_data`

Notes: the stable boundary is the documented equation-system and accessor API.
General finite-field F/R solving and cyclotomic reconstruction are
experimental and may change before v1.0.
